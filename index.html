Why Choose Ceylon Cinnamon?

Ceylon Cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum), also known as "True Cinnamon," is native to Sri Lanka. Unlike the common Cassia cinnamon found in most supermarkets, Ceylon Cinnamon is lighter in color, possesses a delicate, sweet flavor, and is packed with antioxidants.

It contains ultra-low levels of Coumarin, making it safe for daily consumption. Our mission is to educate the world about this golden spice and its myriad of uses in cooking and wellness.
